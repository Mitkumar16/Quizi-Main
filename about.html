About Quizi

Quizi is a modern quiz application that allows users to test their knowledge on a variety of topics.

Quizi is perfect for anyone who wants to test their knowledge, learn new things, or simply have some fun. If you're looking for a challenging and rewarding quiz app, then Quizi is the perfect choice for you.

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to learn and grow, and we hope that Quizi can help you achieve your educational goals.
